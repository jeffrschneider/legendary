Observability & Auditing

Real-time monitoring systems that track agent decisions, flag anomalies, and maintain audit trails for compliance and continuous improvement.

Trust at Scale

Agents operating without governance aren't autonomous; they're unaccountable. One bad decision can cascade into regulatory violations, reputational damage, or operational chaos.

But over-governance kills velocity. The goal isn't to prevent agents from acting. It's to ensure they act within boundaries that protect the organization while enabling speed.

Good governance doesn't slow you down. It lets you move fast with confidence.

Governance Layers

A multi-layered approach that balances control with agility.

Preventive Controls

Input validation, scope limits, and guardrails that prevent agents from taking prohibited actions in the first place.

Detective Controls

Real-time monitoring, anomaly detection, and alerting systems that identify issues as they occur.

Corrective Controls

Rollback mechanisms, incident response procedures, and remediation workflows for when things go wrong.
